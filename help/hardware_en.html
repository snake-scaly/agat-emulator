Supported hardware

  • Original Apple ][ with different memory sizes;
  • Apple ][ Plus including Apple ][ j-Plus;
  • Apple I with or without ACI;
  • Apple //e and Enhanced Apple //e;
  • Agat-7, Agat-9;
  • All standard text and graphic modes including combined one for Apple;
  • Tape interface;
  • Paddle interface;
  • Beeper support;
  • Apple Language and Firmware cards;
  • Shugart Floppy Drive Controller (with 1 or 2 drives) with raw images support;
  • Apple Parallel Printer Interface Card;
  • Microsoft Z80 Softcard;
  • Videx Videoterm card;
  • Thunderclock Plus card;
  • Mockingboard II sound card;
  • Agat-7/9 140K/800K floppy drive controllers with raw images support;
  • Agat-7 xRAM and xROM cards with configurable memory sizes;
  • Agat-9 128K xRAM cards;
  • Agat-9 printer card with built-in Epson FX printer emulation;
  • Agat-9 Nippel Clock card;
  • Apple ][ Memory Expansion áard;
  • RamFactor (with RamCharger) card;
  • Saturn 16/32/64/128K Memory cards;
  • CMS SCSI card with up to 3 emulated hard disks;
  • Printer port/Nippel/Apple mouse;
  • Liberty Drive;
  • Dallas Smartwatch / No-Slot-Clock.
